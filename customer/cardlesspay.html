Use Cardless Withdrawal

How to get cash at an ATM without your Kuda Card.

written by Ora Fakorede

  • Use Cardless Withdrawal to get cash at any cardless-enabled ATM without your Kuda Card.

To set up Cardless Withdrawal:

  1. Open Kuda.

  2. Tap Cards then tap Cardless Withdrawal.
  3. Tap Activate Cardless.
  4. Type in the longest number on the back of Kuda Card then tap Add.


    5. To make a cardless withdrawal:

  5. Type in the amount you want to withdraw and set a four-digit cash-out PIN.
  6. Type in your transaction PIN or use Face ID or your fingerprint to confirm.
  7. Save your withdrawal code and use it to get cash at an ATM within 24 hours.
  • If you don’t use your withdrawal code within 24 hours, it will expire and we’ll move your money back into your account in another 24 hours.
